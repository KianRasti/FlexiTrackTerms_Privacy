Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use FlexiTrack.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: If you use Google Sign-In, we receive your basic profile information (email address and name) strictly for account creation and authentication.
  • Usage Data & Logs: The custom schemas you build and the entries you log are stored securely. Depending on your tier, this data is kept locally on your device or synced to our secure cloud database (Supabase).
  • Media (FlexiCloud): If enabled, images you attach to your logs are securely transmitted and stored in our cloud buckets.
  • Subscription Data: We utilize RevenueCat to verify your subscription status. We do not have access to your credit card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide, maintain, and improve the FlexiTrack app. Specifically:

When you request an "AI Insight", relevant excerpts of your logged data are sent securely to our third-party LLM providers (e.g., OpenAI/GPT models). Your personal tracking data is strictly used to generate your immediate response and is NOT used to train or fine-tune public AI models.
